Tteokbokki

Description

Tteokbokki is one of the most popular Korean street foods in Korea

Ingredients

Main

  • 350g / 12 ounces Korean rice cakes, separated
  • 150g / 5.3 ounces Korean fish cakes, rinsed over
    hot water & cut into bite size pieces
  • 2 cups Korean soup stock (dried kelp and dried anchovy stock),
    use this recipe
  • 60g / 2 ounces onion, thinly sliced

Sauce

  • 3 Tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp raw sugar
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)

Steps

  1. Unless your rice cakes are soft already, soak them in warm water for 10 mins.
  2. Boil the soup stock in a shallow pot over medium high heat and dissolve
    the tteokbokki sauce by stirring it with a spatula.
    Once the seasoned stock is boiling, add the rice cakes,
    fish cakes and onion. Boil them a further 3 to 5 mins until
    the rice cakes are fully cooked. Then, to thicken the sauce
    and to deepen the flavor, simmer it over low heat for a further 2 to 4 mins.
  3. Add the sesame oil, sesame seeds, and green onion then quickly stir.
    Serve warm.
